The Story So Far

OK, so Majiir noticed a while back that Kerbal Space Port was getting increasingly inadequate for the modding community's needs, so he announced a Challenge for someone to develop a new replacement, which he had the resources to sufficiently host.

What with the recent announcement that Curse are taking over mod management and Kerbal Space Port is being closed on the 30th May, interest has spiked in building a new mod hosting platform.

Currently, various efforts are being made by lone coders to build a site, which you can read about on the aforementioned forum thread.

  • At the moment, this site is the most complete and visible effort (because it was made earlier)
  • It has been donated to the community by its author.
  • Its github repo is here, and it's written in Ruby on Rails.
