The Story So Far

Majiir noticed a while back (in february) that Kerbal Space Port was getting increasingly inadequate for the modding community's needs, so he announced a Challenge for someone to develop a new replacement, which he had the resources to sufficiently host.

What with the announcement on May that Curse are taking over mod management and Kerbal Space Port is being closed on the 30th May, interest has spiked in building a new mod hosting platform.

Currently, various parallel efforts are being made to build a site, which you can read about on the aforementioned forum thread, and on the section titled Known Projects Being Worked On, further down.

"What we lack is a cohesive set of core features. In other words, we haven't yet (in my opinion) established a good starting point." - Majiir

Let's change that. Let's build a development roadmap, shall we?
